Meet Jimmy Chavez, REALTOR®

Hi, I’m Jimmy Chavez, a dedicated REALTOR® with Future Realty, proudly serving the communities of Coeur d’Alene, Hayden, Rathdrum, and Post Falls.

My mission is simple — to help families and individuals find the home that truly fits their lifestyle, goals, and future. Whether you’re a first-time buyer, an investor, or looking to sell your home, I’m here to make the process clear, confident, and rewarding.

Why Work With Me

  • Local expertise — I live and work right here in North Idaho.
  • Honest communication and full transparency every step of the way.
  • Backed by the trusted team at Future Realty.
  • A commitment to helping you achieve your real estate goals efficiently and stress-free.

More About Me

I live in Rathdrum, Idaho, with my wife and three children. Outside of real estate, I work at the Post Office and value the importance of community, hard work, and balance. I’ve learned that life’s too short not to pursue what you’re passionate about — and for me, that’s helping others move forward through real estate.
